About

I am Tobore Takpor, a computer science student with a deep passion for cybersecurity, cloud computing, and network defense. My interest in technology started from a curiosity about how systems work and how they can be protected. Over time, that curiosity turned into hands-on experience working on projects that focus on vulnerability assessment, system hardening, and secure infrastructure management. I enjoy building solutions that combine creativity and security, such as automated vulnerability scanners and cloud-based monitoring tools. Outside of academics, I am an active member of tech organizations that promote diversity and inclusion in STEM. My goal is to grow into a cybersecurity engineer who builds secure systems and helps make cybersecurity education more accessible to everyone.
